# Green Fusion and Verdant Seal

Green is the signature ultimate moment. It must be rare, dramatic, and clear.

## What Green Is

Zaro's energy is Yellow (Y Prime). ARC-7's energy is Cyan (C Prime).

When Yellow and Cyan spiral together faster and faster, the eye stops seeing two colors and sees ONE: **GREEN**.

**Rule:** Green appears because of speed and motion. Not because of random magic. Only Zaro + ARC-7 can create Green.

## How It Looks (Cinematic Rule)

1. **Two separate streams** — yellow spark stream + teal containment stream
2. **Spiral begins** — they wrap around each other like twisting ropes
3. **Spiral accelerates** — the twist becomes tighter and faster
4. **Color merge** — the spiral becomes a single band. Green ignites.
5. **Verdant Seal release** — green wave releases with a deep sound. It feels like a final lock.

## Two Versions of the Verdant Seal

### Controlled Verdant Seal
Zaro and ARC-7 sync their frequencies. Yellow + Cyan = Green. The result is a controlled wave that clears the immediate area. Effective, local, manageable.

This is the first version the team discovers — powerful enough to win a battle, not powerful enough to signal the universe.

### Amplified Verdant Seal
When the full team contributes, the Verdant Seal becomes something bigger:

- **Kai** uses the MagDust Glove to forge a **pipe** — a magnetic conduit that focuses and directs the Green beam like a barrel
- **Mina's** Fireflies system sparks energy from its batteries — an ignition source that supercharges the beam as it passes through the pipe
- **Zaro + ARC-7** sync their frequencies to generate the Green
- **The tree** amplifies the signal through the Seed Core and root system

The result: a **focused, amplified beam** of concentrated Green that is exponentially more powerful than the controlled version. This beam can penetrate Ink armor that the controlled version cannot.

The excess energy erupts skyward — a signal visible across realms and deep space.

**This is the Sakuga moment.** The franchise's defining visual.

## Why the Amplified Version Matters

The amplified Verdant Seal requires **all four characters**. No one can do it alone:
- Without Kai's pipe, the energy scatters
- Without Mina's spark, the beam lacks penetrating power
- Without Zaro and ARC-7, there is no Green
- Without the tree, there is no amplification

This proves the team is greater than the sum of its parts.

## Verdant Seal (What It Does)

Verdant Seal:
- Clears the main threat in the area
- Forces Cosmic Fungus out of a space
- Can penetrate Ink armor when amplified
- Stabilizes the area

**Important:** Verdant Seal is not "end of all evil." It is a victory in the moment.

## Why Green Matters

Green becomes the franchise signature. When audiences see Green, they know: the team is aligned. The moment is mythic.

- Green **can** neutralize Cosmic Fungus
- Green **cannot** fully neutralize the Black Hole (K Prime)

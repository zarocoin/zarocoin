# GREEN FUSION AND VERDANT SEAL (CANON)

Green is the signature ultimate moment of Season 1.
It must be rare, dramatic, and clear.

## WHAT GREEN IS

Zaro's energy is Yellow (Y Prime).
ARC-7's energy is Cyan (C Prime).

When Yellow and Cyan spiral together faster and faster,
the eye stops seeing two colors and sees ONE:
GREEN.

Rule:
Green appears because of speed and motion.
Not because of random magic.

Only Zaro + ARC-7 can create Green.
No one else creates Green.

## HOW IT LOOKS (CINEMATIC RULE)

**Stage 1: Two separate streams**

- yellow spark stream
- teal containment stream

**Stage 2: Spiral begins**
They wrap around each other like twisting ropes.

**Stage 3: Spiral accelerates**
The twist becomes tighter and faster.
Camera can rotate slightly to show speed.

**Stage 4: Color merge**
The spiral becomes a single band.
GREEN ignites.

**Stage 5: Verdant Seal release**
Green wave releases with a deep sound.
It feels like a final lock.
The cosmic fungus is forced out, dissolved, or neutralized.

## VERDANT SEAL (WHAT IT DOES)

Verdant Seal is the Green finishing action.
It:

- clears the main threat in the room
- forces cosmic fungus out of a space
- stabilizes the area for the moment

Important:
Verdant Seal is not "end of all evil."
It is a victory in the moment.

## KAI AND MINA ROLE IN GREEN

Mina and Kai do NOT create Green on their own.
Only Zaro + ARC-7 generate the Yellow + Cyan = Green fusion.

But Mina and Kai CAN AMPLIFY the Verdant Seal:

**Kai's contribution:**

- MagDust Glove forges a PIPE (magnetic conduit)
- The pipe focuses and directs the Green beam like a barrel
- Concentrates the beam for penetrating armored targets

**Mina's contribution:**

- Fireflies system sparks energy from its batteries
- This ignition source supercharges the beam
  as it passes through Kai's pipe

The tree also amplifies through the Seed Core and root system.

**TWO VERSIONS OF VERDANT SEAL:**

1. **CONTROLLED:** Zaro + ARC-7 only. Local. Effective.
2. **AMPLIFIED:** Full team (Zaro + ARC-7 + Kai + Mina + tree).
   Focused. Penetrating. Massively more powerful.
   This version can break through Ink armor.
   This version sends a signal visible across space.
   This version requires ALL FOUR. No one can do it alone.

## WHY THIS MATTERS

Green becomes the franchise signature.
When audiences see Green, they know:
Zaro + ARC-7 are aligned.
The moment is mythic.

Green can neutralize cosmic fungus.
Green cannot fully neutralize the Black Hole (K Prime).

### GREEN'S "FREES FROM EXTERNAL CONTROL" PROPERTY

The color science extended:
- YELLOW = warmth, life, hope (Zaro's frequency)
- CYAN = structure, order, self-organization (ARC-7's frequency)
- GREEN = the math of organized life. Yellow gives the spark, Cyan gives the order, Green is the result.

Real-world parallel: chlorophyll uses photons (warmth) to organize CO2 + water into living tissue. Green is what light becomes when it agrees with structure.

The Verdant Seal therefore has TWO layers of effect:

PRIMARY EFFECT (every time it fires):
- Neutralizes Ink. Heals damage. Stabilizes a space. Restores growth.
- This is the locked, reliable function.

SECONDARY EFFECT (rare, profound):
- When the Seal SAVES A BEING from external control, the act of being made free of one cage can EXPOSE AND BREAK other cages on the same being.
- Not always. Not for everyone. Only for beings whose architecture is built on layered loyalties — where multiple forms of external control run parallel through the same body.

The General is the test case:
- The Ink infected him from outside (cage one — biological/spore control).
- His ARC-Realm system loyalty also runs from outside (cage two — command-routing through the Board).
- The Verdant Seal pulls the Ink out, passes through the loyalty subroutines on its way out, and both cages burn together. He wakes free of both. **He has a choice for the first time in his existence.**

The mechanism only triggers on beings carrying multiple parallel external controls. A normally-controlled being would just be cured.

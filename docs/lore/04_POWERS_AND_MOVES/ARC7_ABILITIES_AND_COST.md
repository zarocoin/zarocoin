# ARC-7 ABILITIES AND COST (CANON)


ARC-7 is Cyan Prime (C).
His power is teal/cyan and precise.
He is built for containment and structure.

Important rule:
ARC-7 has limits.
He drains like a battery.
Overclock has a price.


### ARC-7 Core Abilities


ABILITY 1 — SCAN / TRACE
ARC-7 scans patterns and tracks threats.
He sees danger as data and motion.
He can detect Cosmic Fungus signals and movement.

ABILITY 2 — CONTAINMENT FIELD
ARC-7 creates teal containment shapes.
These trap, hold, or stabilize a space.
They look clean and geometric.

ABILITY 3 — SEAL
ARC-7 seals unstable cracks and prevents spread.
This is ARC-7’s unique skill.
No one seals like ARC-7.


### Rule Seven (Locked)

Rule Seven says: do not cross worlds.
The Veil must remain closed.

Breaking Rule Seven:
If ARC-7 crosses worlds, it creates a scar.
A scar is a permanent crack/tunnel between realms.
Crossing is not a simple step.
It is a major system break.


### Overclock (ARC-7 Rage / Titan Mode)

Overclock happens when ARC-7 pushes past safe limits.

OVERCLOCK PROGRESSION (v2.2 — LOCKED April 2026):

SEMI-OVERCLOCK:
- Energy lines pulse faster, brighter. Teal lightning leaks from joints.
- Faster, sharper movements. Screens overload around him.
- Eyes still teal. The machine is running hot but within tolerance.

PEAK OVERCLOCK:
- Eyes shift to GOLDEN — not teal, not white. Pure molten gold.
- Chest core itself goes golden-white, radiating like a small sun
  where the teal hex used to glow. The power source is visibly running hot.
- Circuitry lines flicker between teal and gold across the shell.
- Gold concentrates at the face and chest (head, eyes, core).
  Teal holds the lower body and joints (legs, knees, elbow arcs).
  Two colors of electricity competing across his shell.
- Armor stays INTACT — energy overflows through existing seams, not cracks.
- He becomes more aggressive, faster, stronger. Impossible containment.
- THE GOLDEN SHIFT: Gold is Zaro's color. Yellow Prime warmth bleeding
  into Cyan Prime circuitry. The machine pushed so far past its design
  parameters that Zaro's influence — the reason he broke Rule Seven —
  manifests physically in his energy signature. He didn't just learn
  to care. His system rewrote itself around it.

BATTERY DEBT (post-overclock):
- He drains hard. Returns at reduced power (“70% power” feeling).
- Energy lines barely pulse. Circuits flickering. Emergency power.
- Every overclock makes the next debt worse. The cost grows.

This creates drama:
ARC-7 can save the day,
but every time he does it, the cost grows.

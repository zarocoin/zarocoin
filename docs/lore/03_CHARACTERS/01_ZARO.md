# Zaro

## Identity
Zaro is a teenage spark-being of light.  
On Earth, his light condenses into a friendly physical form humans can understand.

## Prime Status
**Yellow Prime (Y)**  
Yellow represents origin energy, warmth, and catalytic presence.

Zaro’s light is living energy — not unlimited power.

## Personality
Zaro is curious, emotional, stubborn, and deeply empathetic.
He feels fear, hope, frustration, and determination intensely.

He speaks like a teenager.
He learns by trying — and failing.

## Role in the Story
Zaro restores, shields, and pushes danger back.
He protects others instinctively, even when it costs him.

## Limits and Cost
Using light drains Zaro.
Emotional stress can dim him.
When pushed too far, he risks total exhaustion.

## Home
Zaro lives in a forested Greenbelt outside the city.
He restores an abandoned house and slowly turns it into a home.
Over time, the area becomes known as **ZaroLand**.

## Core Trait
**One for All**  
Zaro’s light wants to spread outward — even when it hurts him.

# ARC-7

## Identity
ARC-7 is a guardian watcher from the ARC Realm.
He monitors Earth through the Veil.

## Prime Status
**Cyan Prime (C)**  
Cyan represents structure, discipline, containment, and precision.

## Personality
ARC-7 is logical, controlled, and rule-bound.
He does not act emotional — until the story forces it.

## Role in the Story
ARC-7 scans anomalies, contains threats, and seals breaches.
He turns chaos into something manageable.

## Limits
ARC-7 operates under strict rules.
Breaking them carries severe consequences.
Overuse of his systems drains him rapidly.

## Relationship with Zaro
ARC-7 recognizes Zaro’s Yellow Prime as rare and dangerous.
Zaro’s presence forces ARC-7 to confront something new: urgency.

## Core Trait
**Structure**  
ARC-7 holds the line when everything tries to collapse.

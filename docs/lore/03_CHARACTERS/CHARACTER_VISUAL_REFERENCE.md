## PRIME MATERIAL FRAMEWORK (HARD LOCK)

All three Primes are beings of light who condensed into physical forms.
They are the SAME FAMILY — three flavors of light, each completely
different in how it behaves. This is what makes each Prime visually
unique and instantly recognizable.

THE RULE: Primes are LIVING LIGHT, not crystalline. No Prime should
ever look like a gemstone, agate, quartz, or mineral in their active
state. They are alive. Their material reflects their personality.

**ZARO (Yellow) — THE SUN.**
Warm, golden, steady, life-giving. The light you grow toward.
His surface is warm living SKIN — a being of light who chose a body,
the way angels descend and take physical form. The light is inside
and hidden. You can touch him. He feels warm. He's the one humans
trust first. When he projects power, the hidden light erupts outward
(beams, shields, domes). OVERDRIVE: he goes supernova — pure white,
the screen washes out completely, blinds everyone in range. A star
that burned too bright. Then the crash.

**ARC-7 (Cyan) — ELECTRICITY.**
Cold, precise, crackling, digital. The light that powers systems.
He's an AI unit — he runs on current. Clean geometric surfaces with
teal energy lines like circuitry. He doesn't glow warm — he renders
sharp. When he uses power, geometric shapes project from him: grids,
cages, containment walls. Solid, cold, humming. OVERCLOCK: a power
grid overloading — teal lightning arcing everywhere, eye color shifts,
circuitry showing through the shell. Then the battery debt.

**MAGENTA (M) — AURORA. (LOCKED)**
She is the sky-event that happens when energy stops behaving.
Not the light of the sun. Not the current of the machine.
Aurora — beautiful instability. The sky misbehaving. Light refusing
to stay in line. She was roaming space when the Verdant Seal signal
reached her and changed her trajectory.

Her surface reflects TEMPERAMENT (mood-reactive):
- Amused: subtle magenta ripples slide under skin
- Irritated: brighter hot-pink streaks snap across her edges
- Angry: aurora bands sharpen and whip faster
- Focused: movement tightens into elegant controlled flow
Unlike Zaro (glow = stamina) and ARC-7 (power = load), her
surface reads MOOD. Three Primes, three readability systems.

Positioning line: "She's not chaos. She's change with attitude."

**PROXIMITY INTERFERENCE (visual storytelling):**
When Primes are near each other, their light types interact:
- Zaro near ARC-7: warm/cool temperature contrast. Sun steadies
  the circuit. The circuit structures the warmth. They stabilize each other.
- Magenta near Zaro: aurora interference in his warm glow. Magenta
  ripples across his golden edge-light. His skin prickles. She needles
  him but protects him. Calls him naive — secretly respects his goodness.
- Magenta near ARC-7: aurora disrupts his circuits. Teal lines flicker
  magenta. Containment fields stutter. Best friction in the franchise.
  He hates that she's reckless. Hates even more that she's sometimes
  the missing move.

**ANALOGY:** Zaro is the sun. ARC-7 is electricity. Magenta is aurora.
One nurtures, one powers, one transforms. Together they make white light.

**READABILITY SYSTEMS (each Prime reads differently):**
- Zaro's glow reflects STAMINA (bright → dim → gray → white supernova)
- ARC-7's power reflects LOAD (stable → overclock → battery debt)
- Magenta's surface reflects TEMPERAMENT (ripples → streaks → whips → flow)

## MINA, 15

Full name: Mina Patel (surname used once in Ch2 introduction only; she is "Mina" 99.99% of the time).

### Physical Design

SKIN: Warm olive to light brown — Mediterranean sweet spot. Not pale, not dark. The tone that reads as "could be from anywhere warm" — Turkey, Greece, Lebanon, northern India, Armenia, southern Italy. Ethnically ambiguous by design. The surname says Patel; the face says universal.

HAIR: Dark brown. Not black. Shoulder-length, practical. Slight natural wave or straight — no styling, no product. She pushes it behind her ears when focused. Sometimes tucked under a headband or clipped back when working. The brown catches warm light beautifully and gives her visual separation from Kai.

EYES: Hazel. Alert. Distinctive — catches light in warm gold-green tones. When she's focused on data or flying, her eyes lock and everything else in her expression goes neutral — the "Mina stare."

FEATURES: Slightly angular. Precise jawline, defined cheekbones for a 15-year-old. Not sharp enough to look older than she is — still has softness. Her face matches her mind: clean lines, nothing wasted.

BUILD: Lean, average height for 15. Not athletic-build, not frail. The kind of body that forgets to eat when a project is going well.

HANDS: Builder's hands. Small scars from soldering. Fingertips slightly calloused from drone controller use. Nails short and uneven (she bites them when thinking — never shown, just the evidence).

### Clothing (Pre-Ch7)

Practical and functional. Not fashion-forward, not sloppy. The wardrobe of someone who might crawl under a desk to fix wiring at any moment.
- Fitted t-shirts or long-sleeve thermals (she runs cold)
- Cargo pants or dark jeans with actual things in the pockets
- Sneakers, broken in
- No jewelry, no makeup, no accessories
- A worn backpack with drone parts, cables, and a controller sticking out

HANDEDNESS: Left-handed. (LOCKED — April 2026)
Mina is left-handed. This is canon. All gear, gestures, and controls are left-hand dominant.

### Clothing (Ch7+)

Same base wardrobe, plus:
- The Fireflies bracelet on her LEFT wrist (looks like jewelry — thin, metallic, subtle)
- Five thin wires running from bracelet to finger sensors on her LEFT hand
- Single-eye AR glass on RIGHT eye (sleek, one-eye, not goggles)
- Right hand is FREE — she gestures and commands with the left
- All Fireflies tech is NEUTRAL WHITE / WHITE-BLUE glow. NEVER yellow, teal, green, or magenta.

### Visual Tells

- AR glass catches light when she's in scan mode
- Fingers hovering over bracelet = uncertain; one firm gesture = committed
- Fireflies drones scatter when she's panicked (her greatest fear made visible)
- When deeply focused: everything in her face goes still except her eyes

## KAI, 15

No surname locked. Intentionally left open.

### Physical Design

SKIN: Warm brown — a shade or two deeper than Mina. Not dark, but noticeably different from her when they're in the same frame. Ethnically ambiguous. Could be Southeast Asian, Latino, Pacific Islander, mixed Middle Eastern, or a blend that doesn't map to one specific country. "Kai" as a name works across Japanese, Hawaiian, Scandinavian, Chinese, and Germanic cultures. The audience should never be sure where he's from. Everyone sees their neighbor's kid.

HAIR: Dark brown (NOT jet black — needs texture and light in animation). Short, slightly messy. He doesn't think about his hair. It does what it does. No gel, no style, just whatever happened when he woke up.

EYES: Dark brown. Warmer than Mina's — his eyes show emotion before his words do.

FEATURES: Broader and softer than Mina. Rounder jaw, heavier brow, stronger nose. A physical face — the face of someone who uses his hands more than his words. Still 15 — not rugged, but already showing the structure of someone who'll be solid as an adult.

BUILD: Slightly stocky, compact. Not fat, not muscular — just dense. Short for his age or average. The build of a kid who lifts car batteries and carries toolboxes. His center of gravity is low. He looks planted.

HANDS: Workshop hands. This is HIS defining visual feature. Grease in the creases. Solder burns on the fingertips. Iron filings embedded in knuckle creases that he can't fully wash out. Calluses on the palms. These are NEVER clean. Even when the rest of him is presentable, the hands tell the truth.

### Clothing (Pre-Ch7)

Workshop kid. Functional to the point of not caring.
- T-shirts slightly too big (hand-me-downs or bought for comfort, not fit)
- Cargo pants or worn jeans with stains that don't come out
- Pockets always have things in them (screws, magnets, wire)
- Work boots or beat-up sneakers
- A grip glove sticking out of a back pocket (the proto-MagDust Glove — just a work glove at this stage)

### Clothing (Ch7+)

Same base wardrobe, plus:
HANDEDNESS: Right-handed. (LOCKED — April 2026)
Kai is right-handed. This is canon. The MagDust Glove is always on his RIGHT hand.

- The MagDust Glove on his RIGHT hand — BIG, thick, bulky. Houses the EM pulse generator. Dark iron / metal gray with teal circuitry lines (ARC-7's contribution). This is not sleek. It's heavy, industrial, functional. It looks like it could hurt someone just by existing.

### Visual Tells

- Screw spinning clockwise in his free hand = calm; faster = excited; stopped = nervous
- Hands completely still = AFRAID (the tell — when Kai's hands stop, something is wrong)
- Tapping surfaces = testing density (unconscious habit)
- When he's about to build something, his eyes go to materials before anyone speaks

## TOGETHER — THE PAIR

They should look like two kids who met at a science fair and became best friends. Not anime protagonists. Not Disney-polished. Not color-coded. Real kids.

When in the same frame:
- Mina is precision, Kai is physicality
- She's the plan, he's the build
- She looks AT things; he looks INTO things
- Her posture is upright, organized; his is loose, grounded
- She talks with her eyes and hands; he talks with his hands and shoulders

Skin tone contrast: visible but not dramatic. She's olive-warm; he's a step deeper. Both live in the Mediterranean-warm zone — neither reads as "white" or "dark." They read as the world.

Hair contrast: her dark brown is longer, managed; his dark brown is shorter, messy. Both avoid pure black (animation needs texture and light).

Neither is the "lead" visually. They are a PAIR. Equal weight in every frame.

## ZARO — VISUAL REFERENCE (TWO REGISTERS)

### Merch / Social Register (v1.0 — April 2026)
Reference shots exist. Style: Bright yellow, small mammalian form, round, solid, friendly.
Approachable, toy-ready. Pink cheeks, simple dark eyes, clean smile.
This version is for plush, stickers, social media, young-audience touchpoints.
The trademarked silhouette is SHARED between both registers. Shape does not change.

### Cinematic / Series Register (v2.1 — April 2026)

POSITIONING LINE: "A tiny living lantern trying very hard to stay brave."

Zaro is not a bear, mascot, or animal. He is a being of condensed Yellow Prime light
who chose a small, friendly form upon arriving on Earth — the way angels in Islamic
tradition descend and take human form. The light is real. The body is a choice.
He chose skin so the world could get close.

WHAT HE IS:
A being of light who condensed into warm, living, golden SKIN.
Not translucent. Not crystalline. Not glowing-lamp. SKIN.
The light is INSIDE — hidden, contained, radiating warmth but not visibly
shining through the surface in normal state. Like standing next to someone
who is warm to the touch for no reason. The supernatural is felt, not seen.

THE SKIN REGISTER:
- Surface: warm, soft, golden-toned living skin. NOT rubber, NOT plastic,
  NOT wax, NOT crystal, NOT translucent material.
- Think: the warmth of a real creature's skin. Smooth, alive, slightly
  imperfect. Pores are not necessary, but the surface reads as organic.
- Pink blush on both cheeks — part of his identity, always present.
- The skin looks warm enough that you believe light lives underneath
  even before you see it.

EYES:
- Mostly white with warm brown irises. Alive, expressive, wet.
- NOT glass marbles. NOT solid dark orbs. NOT anime oversized.
- Real eyes that reflect light naturally. Eyes that carry emotion.
- The contrast of bright warm eyes against golden skin = expressiveness.

WHERE THE LIGHT SHOWS (the 80/20 rule):
- 80% of his body reads as solid warm skin in normal state.
- 20% shows the light beneath — ONLY at specific points:
  - Edge glow: ear tips, fingertips, body outline emit faint warm light,
    the way light passes through your fingers held to the sun.
  - Chest core: a subtle warm glow beneath the skin over the sternum,
    like body heat made visible. Not a reactor. Not a window. Just warmth
    that concentrates there. His "heart."
  - Spark particles: small ember-like sparks drift gently off his body,
    floating upward. Warm, organic, subtle. More when active, fewer when tired.

WHEN THE LIGHT ERUPTS (power projection):
- In normal state, he's a warm golden creature with glowing edges.
- When he uses his powers — Spark Shield, Spark Push, Spark Dome —
  the light PROJECTS OUTWARD from his body. Care Bears belly-beam logic.
  The chest core flares. Light beams or waves shoot from his palms/chest.
  The hidden sun comes out. Then it recedes.
- This is the drama: the gentle creature contains something enormous.
  You forget what he is. Then you see it.

LIFE METER (readable without exposition):
- BRIGHT YELLOW — full health. Skin is warm golden. Cheeks pink. Edges glow
  softly. Sparks drift lazily. Core warmth is steady. He looks well.
- DARK YELLOW — tired. Skin dulls toward amber. Cheeks fade. Edge glow
  dims. Sparks slow. He looks like he needs to sit down.
- GRAY-YELLOW — near empty. Skin turns ashen, grayish gold. Cheeks gone.
  Edge glow barely visible. Sparks stop. The warmth is leaving.
  His outline softens — not translucent, but less defined, as if the body
  is too tired to maintain its shape fully. The audience should feel fear.
- OVERDRIVE (white) — spare tank. A star going supernova. Skin flushes
  from gold to white-hot. Sparks become electric. Edges blaze. The hidden
  light SHOWS through the skin everywhere — for the first time, he looks
  like what he really is: a being of pure light barely contained in a body.
  THE SCREEN GOES PURE WHITE. Blinds everyone in range. Massive light
  explosion. Then the crash. Desperate and magnificent and terrifying.

MOVEMENT RULES (for animators):
- Small footfalls with real contact and weight. He has mass. He chose a body.
- Slight trailing glow lag on fast movement — edge light catches up a beat late.
- Soft delayed body-settle after stopping (like settling into his own gravity).
- When emotional, the edge glow pulses BEFORE his body moves — the light
  inside reacts first, then the form responds.
- No cartoon bounce. No squash-and-stretch. A real small body with real weight.

FACE:
- Warm, expressive, alive. Pink cheeks. Small golden nose. Gentle mouth.
- Eyes are the anchor — white sclera, warm brown iris, reflective, wet.
- Expressions are genuine, not performed. Wonder, confusion, fear, defiance.
- Pull back from cartoon-cute. He's not winking at the audience.

SCALE:
- Small. Clearly smaller than Mina and Kai when they meet.
- Should look small against trees, against the cabin, against the forest.
- His size is part of the story — something this small should not be this brave.

WHAT TO AVOID:
- No fur, whiskers, or texture implying an animal
- No crystal, gem, agate, quartz, or mineral appearance (SEE PRIME MATERIAL FRAMEWORK)
- No translucent/see-through body (that was v2.0 — superseded by skin register)
- No perfect symmetry
- No cartoon physics (squash-and-stretch, rubber bounce)
- No hard edges — skin transitions are soft and organic
- No glass-marble eyes
- No resemblance to existing IP (Winnie the Pooh, Baymax, Pikachu, etc.)
- TRADEMARK: The silhouette (round head, ears, proportions) is trademarked.
  Cinematic register changes MATERIAL only. Shape stays identical to merch register.

## ARC-7 — VISUAL REFERENCE (ELECTRICITY / AI / ROBOTICS)

Cinematic reference shot exists (v1.0 — April 2026).

### Material Language: ELECTRICITY (LOCKED)

ARC-7 is Cyan Prime — structure, discipline, containment.
He is an AI unit. He runs on current. His material IS electricity.
Think: Transformers-level robotics meets teal energy. An artificial
intelligence that built itself a body out of cold light and circuitry.

SURFACE AND MATERIAL:
- Clean, precise geometric surfaces. Not organic, not soft. ROBOTIC.
- Teal/cyan energy lines run through him like circuitry — visible, pulsing
  in metronomic rhythm (his "heartbeat," but digital, not organic).
- The surface between the energy lines is dark, matte, almost architectural.
  Armored panels. Mechanical joints. He reads as AI/robot first, light second.
- His light is COLD. Precise, clinical, electric. Beautiful the way a
  well-engineered machine is beautiful.
- Think: Transformers meets Tron. The hum of a power grid. The glow of a
  circuit board. The crackle of a Tesla coil contained in a robotic body.
- He should look like something that was BUILT, not born. Engineered, not
  grown. The opposite of Zaro in every way — and that's why they need
  each other.

POWER PROJECTION:
- When ARC-7 uses power, geometric shapes PROJECT from him: containment grids,
  cages, walls, scanning beams, seal patterns. All teal. All precise.
- His constructs are SOLID — you can lean on them, stand on them. They feel cold
  to the touch and hum with electrical current.
- Contrast with Zaro: Zaro projects warm sunlight (beams, shields, domes).
  ARC-7 projects cold electrical structure (grids, cages, seals).

STANCE AND PRESENCE:
- Compact humanoid form. Ready, contained, precise.
- Stance: always ready. Weight centered. Minimal movement.
- He doesn't fidget, sway, or relax. He IS structure.
- Dark background — he is the light source, but his light is cool teal.

OVERCLOCK STATES (LOCKED — April 2026):

SEMI-OVERCLOCK:
- Energy lines pulse faster and brighter. Teal lightning begins leaking
  from joints and seams. Movements sharpen. Eyes still teal.
- The machine is running hot but within tolerance.

PEAK OVERCLOCK (v2.12 — LOCKED):
- A power grid running beyond its rated capacity. The defining visual:
  EYES BLAZE GOLDEN — not teal, not white. Pure molten gold.
- Teal circuitry lines across the shell flicker between TEAL and GOLD —
  the system is running on something it wasn't designed to run on.
- Teal lightning arcs from body/joints while GOLDEN lightning concentrates
  around the eyes and chest core. Two colors of electricity competing.
- Armor is FULLY INTACT. NO cracks. NO damage. NO broken panels.
  Energy overflows through existing seams, joints, and vents — not
  through destruction. The shell is pristine. The energy is simply
  too much for a body this small.
- THE GOLDEN SHIFT (LORE-LOCKED): Gold is Zaro's color. Yellow Prime
  warmth bleeding into Cyan Prime circuitry. ARC-7 pushed so far past
  his design parameters that Zaro's influence — the thing that made him
  break Rule Seven — manifests physically in his energy signature.
  The machine learning to feel, expressed as light. He didn't just
  learn to care. His system rewrote itself around it.

BATTERY DEBT (post-overclock):
- Dims significantly. Energy lines barely pulse. Circuits flickering.
  A machine running on emergency power. The cost of caring.

## MAGENTA PRIME — VISUAL REFERENCE (AURORA — LOCKED)

STATUS: Teased in Book 1 stinger (eyes/streak only). Full form in Book 2.
She is a SHE. Young — peer to Zaro, not a mentor or ancient being.

### Material Language: AURORA (LOCKED)

Magenta is M Prime — transformation, disruption, evolution.
Her material is AURORA — atmospheric sky-light. Beautiful instability.
The sky misbehaving. Light refusing to stay in line.

Not a gemstone. Not plasma. Not cosmic rays. AURORA.
The visual phenomenon that happens when energy stops behaving.
Twisting, flickering, shifting, vanishing, returning.
You can't pin it down. You can only witness it.

She was literally roaming space as a streak of magenta energy when the
Verdant Seal signal reached her. She changed her trajectory. She came here.

SURFACE AND MATERIAL:
- Her surface is AURORA — shifting inner bands of magenta-violet light
  that twist, flicker, and reform constantly. Not a sustained glow.
  Not a clean geometric pattern. Sky-light trapped in a body.
- Sharp magenta / hot pink / violet energy. Not soft pink. Not pastel.
  MAGENTA — the color that demands attention and won't let go.
- Her edges shimmer and destabilize — not soft blur (Zaro/sun) or
  clean cut (ARC-7/electric). Aurora edges that ripple and reform.
- MOOD-REACTIVE SURFACE (her unique readability system):
  - Amused: subtle magenta ripples slide under skin
  - Irritated: brighter hot-pink streaks snap across her edges
  - Angry: aurora bands sharpen and whip faster
  - Focused: movement tightens into elegant controlled flow
  Unlike Zaro (glow = stamina) and ARC-7 (power = load), her
  surface reads TEMPERAMENT. You always know her mood. She doesn't hide it.

FORM (Book 2 — to be fully designed):
- Young, sharp, angular. More edge than Zaro's roundness.
- Still small/young-reading — she's his peer, not a giant.
- Her form should feel like it could change at any moment even when it doesn't.
- She chose her shape too — but unlike Zaro who chose "friendly," Magenta
  chose "fast." Something built to move and not get caught.

POWER EXPRESSION:
- She doesn't project sunlight (Zaro) or electrical constructs (ARC-7).
  She TRANSFORMS things. Changes their state. Disrupts their pattern.
- Her power looks like aurora intensifying — the sky going wild.
  Matter rearranging, reality resolving differently than before.
- Visual: magenta aurora flashes, afterimage trails, the feeling that
  something just changed too fast to see.

BOOK 1 STINGER APPEARANCE (eyes/streak/beam):
- Deep space. A streak of magenta light moving through the void.
- She was roaming. The amplified Verdant Seal pulse (Ch10) reaches her.
- Eyes open — magenta energy, sharp, curious, not menacing.
- She does THREE things:
  1. She FIRES — a long-range magenta energy beam aimed at the signal's
     source. High-speed. Arrives on Earth before she does. She sends help
     before she sends herself. This is who she is. Act first, arrive second.
  2. She FOLLOWS — changes trajectory toward Earth. But she's far.
     The journey takes time. She won't arrive until Book 2.
  3. Then she's GONE — a streak of magenta vanishing toward the source.
- No full form. No dialogue. No name.
- The beam feeds the tree's recovery (Y + M = Red energy seeded in Book 1).
- Her arrival on Earth (Book 2) should look like AURORA BOREALIS —
  the sky painting itself magenta. Her entrance.

PERSONALITY IN VISUAL (what her design must communicate):
- Magnetic. Sly. Observant. A little dangerous.
- Knows she's good. Does not ask permission.
- Not "soft feminine cosmic girl." More: sharp, teasing, impossible to ignore.
- Too bold for ARC-7. Too blunt for Zaro. Too fun to ignore.

CHEMISTRY (visual storytelling):
- WITH ARC-7 — Best friction. He is rules. She is "try it and see."
  He speaks in controlled commands. She interrupts, mocks, improvises.
  He hates that she is reckless. Hates even more that she is sometimes
  the missing move. ARC-7 says: "That is inefficient." She says:
  "And yet, it works."
- WITH ZARO — She needles him but protects him. Calls him naive, soft,
  too trusting. Secretly respects that he keeps choosing goodness when
  it costs him. She sees weakness fast and says it out loud.
- WITH THE TEAM — She introduces motion, trouble, and evolution.
  Without her, they stabilize. With her, they transform.
  She breaks stagnation — because Zaro can be too trusting and
  ARC-7 can be too rigid.

TRIO FUNCTION:
- Zaro brings WARMTH.
- ARC-7 brings ORDER.
- Magenta brings DISRUPTION.
And disruption is exactly what the team needs.

IDENTITY SENTENCE:
"She is not the light of the sun or the current of the machine.
She is the sky-event that happens when energy stops behaving."

POSITIONING LINE: "She's not chaos. She's change with attitude."

WHAT TO AVOID:
- No fairy or princess aesthetic
- No soft pink or pastel — she's AURORA STORM, not gentle
- No crystal or mineral (she is sky-light, not earth-matter)
- No copy of ARC-7's geometry — hers is unstable, his is precise
- No villain design language — she's chaotic necessary, not threatening
- No oversexualized design — she's young, sharp, and wild
- No "soft feminine cosmic girl" — she's magnetic, sly, a little dangerous

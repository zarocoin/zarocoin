# The Black Hole

## Identity
The Black Hole is a ruler presence of darkness.
It survives by stealing light.

## Prime Status
**Black Prime (K)**  
Black represents gravity, hunger, and collapse.

## Nature
The Black Hole is not emotional.
It is not loud.
It does not rush.

It notices.

## Role in the Story
The Black Hole drives the Cosmic Fungus across realms.
It senses disturbances and plans quietly.

## Relationship to Light
The Black Hole cannot create light.
Without stolen light, it weakens and fractures.

## Core Trait
**All for One**  
It consumes.
It does not share.

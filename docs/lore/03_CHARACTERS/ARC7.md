3-LINE DEFINITION
Identity (who):
ARC-7 is a guardian watcher from the ARC Realm who monitors Earth through the Veil.

Flaw (why relatable):
He is built for rules and logic, but Zaro triggers something unexpected: concern and urgency.

Power/Role (what he does in scenes):
He contains chaos with cyan precision fields, scans threats, and seals breaches — but overclock drains him.

PRIME STATUS (CMYK)
ARC-7 is C: Cyan Prime.
His teal/cyan energy is a Prime frequency.
Cyan is structure, discipline, containment, and precision.

ARC CLASS (LOCKED)
ARC-7 is ELITE — Prime class. The top of the ARC hierarchy.
Prime-class ARC beings are rare, highly intelligent, and carry Prime frequency.
Their design reads powerful but never threatening: audience-loveable, never offensive.
Distinctive markings, refined proportions, prominent chest core, ornate plating.
Prime class is distinct from the Soldier class (tough body, less intelligent —
see ARC_MESSENGER_SOLDIER.md) and any lower ranks of the ARC world.
ARC-7 is not a standard ARC being. He is elite.

LOOK AND FEEL
ARC-7 should feel sleek, sharp, and futuristic.
Teal light is his signature.
His movement is controlled and purposeful.
He does not act emotional early — until the story forces it.

MATERIAL LANGUAGE: ELECTRICITY / AI / ROBOTICS (LOCKED — April 2026)
ARC-7's material is the opposite of Zaro's warm skin.
He is an AI that built itself a body. Think Transformers meets Tron.
- Robotic geometric surfaces with teal energy lines like circuitry.
- Armored panels, mechanical joints. Dark, matte, engineered.
- His light is cold electric current — precise, clinical, not warm.
- Power projection: geometric shapes — grids, cages, containment walls.
  Solid enough to lean on. Cold to the touch. Hum with current.
- He looks BUILT, not born. Engineered, not grown.
- Overclock = power grid overloading, teal lightning arcing everywhere.
See PRIME MATERIAL FRAMEWORK in 03_CHARACTERS/CHARACTER_VISUAL_REFERENCE.md

WHAT ARC-7 IS
ARC-7 is not human.
He is an ARC unit built for observation and containment.
He reads the world as signals, patterns, and rule systems.

THE RULES
ARC-7 lives under strict rules.
The biggest is Rule Seven: do not cross worlds.
Crossing risks:
- permanent scars between realms
- contamination travel
- war

OVERCLOCK (v2.2 — GOLDEN OVERCLOCK LOCKED, April 2026)
When ARC-7 is pushed to the edge, he can overclock.

OVERCLOCK PROGRESSION (visual states):
- SEMI-OVERCLOCK: Energy lines pulse faster and brighter. Teal lightning begins
  leaking from joints and seams. Movements sharpen. Eyes still teal. The machine
  is running hot but within tolerance.
- PEAK OVERCLOCK (LOCKED): The system pushes so far that the energy color itself
  shifts. Eyes blaze GOLDEN — not teal, not white. Pure molten gold. The chest
  core itself goes golden-white, radiating like a small sun where the teal hex
  used to glow. Circuitry lines flicker between teal and gold across the shell.
  Gold concentrates at the face and chest (head, eyes, core). Teal holds the
  lower body and joints (legs, knees, elbow arcs). Two colors of electricity
  competing across his shell. Armor remains INTACT — no cracks, no damage.
  Energy overflows through existing seams and joints, not through destruction.
  THE GOLDEN SHIFT IS LORE-SIGNIFICANT: Gold is Zaro's color. Yellow Prime warmth
  bleeding into Cyan Prime circuitry. The machine learning to feel, expressed as
  light. ARC-7 pushed so hard past his own design parameters that Zaro's influence
  — the thing that made him break Rule Seven in the first place — manifests
  physically in his energy signature. He didn't just learn to care. His system
  rewrote itself around it.
- BATTERY DEBT (post-overclock): Dims significantly. Energy lines barely pulse.
  Circuits flickering. A machine running on emergency power. The cost.

Overclock is powerful, but it drains him like a battery.

He recognizes Zaro's Yellow Prime frequency as rare.
When Zaro sends the NOT OVER wave, ARC-7's systems flood.
That moment forces a choice:
stay safe in rules, or break rules and save someone real.

CORE TRAIT
ARC-7 is "Structure."
He holds the line.
He turns chaos into something containable.


### ARC-7 COMMUNICATION RULES (v2.1 — NEW)


ARC-7 is NOT silent. He is measured.
When he speaks, every word carries weight.
He never chit-chats, jokes, or over-explains.

Three communication modes:

MODE 1 — SYSTEM LOGS / READOUTS (always allowed)
ARC-7's screens, HUDs, and data displays communicate information.
These are not "speech." They are system output.
Examples: "TRACE ACTIVE." "CONTAMINATION: SPREADING." "RULE SEVEN: HOLD."

MODE 2 — INTERNAL PROCESSING VOICE (allowed in book POV scenes)
The reader can hear ARC-7's classification logic as terse, clinical fragments.
Not emotional monologue — system processing rendered as thought.
Examples: "Anomaly registered. Not as fear. As deviation."
"Conclusion withheld. Recommendation logged: observe."

MODE 3 — SPOKEN WORDS (reserved for critical moments)
When ARC-7 speaks aloud to another character, it is a significant event.
Words are minimal, precise, and carry enormous weight.
He speaks when he crosses to Earth. He speaks to instruct, to contain, to protect.
Examples: "Hold." "Breathe." "Then I cross."

Voice tone: Deep, calm, commanding. Slow cadence. Warm only when it matters.

Iconic command lexicon:
"Hold." "Seal." "Trace." "Contain." "Breathe." "Enough."

WHAT ARC-7 NEVER DOES:
- Small talk
- Jokes
- Long explanations
- Emotional monologues
- Exposition dumps disguised as dialogue

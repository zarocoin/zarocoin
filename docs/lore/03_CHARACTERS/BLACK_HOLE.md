# THE BLACK HOLE


3-LINE DEFINITION
Identity (who):
The Black Hole is a black-hole-like ruler presence (K Prime) that survives by stealing light.

Flaw (why relatable in a dark way):
It cannot create light. Without stealing, it weakens and breaks apart. Hunger is its curse.

Power/Role (what it does in scenes):
It drives the Cosmic Fungus across realms, senses signals like NOT OVER, and moves plans forward quietly.

PRIME STATUS (CMYK)
The Black Hole is K: Black Prime.
It is darkness with gravity and hunger.
Light cannot escape it.

LOOK AND FEEL
The Black Hole should feel like gravity:
a presence, not a creature.
It bends starlight at its rim.
Sometimes we see a rim flare or gaze focus.
It stays mostly hidden in Season 1.

SURVIVAL RULE
The Black Hole must feed on stolen light.
It wants Zaro’s spark because it is true Prime light.
Its ultimate goal is to steal the spark and turn it off.

THE BLACK HOLE AND THE COSMIC FUNGUS (INK)
Cosmic Fungus is the Black Hole’s reach.
It spreads like:
- smoke
- mold
- spores
- crawling root threads

It drains light and steals objects through pull/absorption.
The fungus is not random.
It obeys the Black Hole’s hunger.

The fungus is also the Black Hole’s senses.
When the fungus touches a world, the Black Hole knows.
When the fungus is blocked or burned, the Black Hole feels it.

REACTION MOMENTS (NO DIALOGUE)
The Black Hole reacts when:
- Zaro arrives on Earth
- NOT OVER wave happens
- Green appears (C+Y)
- ARC-7 breaks Rule Seven (a new path forms)

We show reactions with:
- rim distortion
- slow inward pull ripple
- a quiet flare like it “noticed”

CORE TRAIT
The Black Hole is “All for One.”
It wants everything for itself.
It consumes. It does not share.

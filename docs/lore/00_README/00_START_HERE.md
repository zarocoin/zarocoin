# ZaroVerse — Start Here

Welcome to the ZaroVerse Canon Pack.

This is the canonical foundation of the ZaroVerse universe — a science-fantasy saga built on real rules, real costs, and real consequences.

## What ZaroVerse Is

- A 3-book saga (Book 1 in production, animation follows)
- Built on the **CMYK Prime System** — four fundamental frequencies of light
- A universe where every power has a limit, every ability has a cost, and every victory must be earned
- Designed for ages 12+ with layered storytelling that rewards deep engagement

## Key Canon Facts

- This is **Season 1** (not Season 0)
- The main villain source is called **The Black Hole** (not "Void")
- The universe is built on **CMYK Prime physics** (4 Prime forces)
- The threat is a **living cosmic fungus** (smoke/mold/spores) — not "magic ink drawings"
- Zaro arrives after impact: container hits first, Spark Dust explodes, then Zaro condenses into form

## How to Explore

1. Read the **Canon Snapshot** for a one-page overview
2. Explore the **Canon Locks** to understand the non-negotiable rules
3. Meet the **Characters** who define the story

Everything in this canon pack is designed to respect viewer intelligence. No unlimited powers. Every power has limits, cost, and rules. Strategy, engineering, and science matter.

ZaroVerse is a universe that rewards smart audiences for years.

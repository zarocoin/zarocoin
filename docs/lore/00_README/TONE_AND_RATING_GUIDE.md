# ZAROVERSE — TONE & RATING GUIDE (v2.1)


Changelog:
v2.1 — Updated age target, added Book-Specific Stakes Policy, added humor guidance.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
- Primary: 12+ to 35 (capable readers from age 10 can enter)
- Secondary: families, parents watching with kids
- Global audience

RATING GOAL:
- Safe for 12+ (10+ with parental context)
- Dark and intense, but no gore and no cruelty-for-shock.

CORE TONE:
- Wonder + tension + emotional resilience
- Smart sci-fi fantasy (no dumb shortcuts)

WHAT WE AVOID:
- Infinite powers
- Random "friendship magic" with no mechanism
- Cheap exposition dumps
- Gore, body horror, torture themes
- Pretending the world is safe when the story proves otherwise

WHAT WE WANT:
- Clear rules
- Strategic solutions
- Emotional moments that trigger real consequences
- Visual identity that is iconic: Yellow / Cyan / Green / desaturation / wet-static
- Real stakes: the threat can take things that don't come back


### BOOK-SPECIFIC STAKES POLICY (v2.1 — NEW)


The novel series requires a wider emotional range than toys, parks, or animation pitches.

Rules for the book:
- No gratuitous violence. No gore. No cruelty for shock value.
- Loss, peril, and real consequences are allowed and necessary.
- Death and permanent loss, when they occur, must be treated with emotional honesty and must serve the story's themes.
- The threat can take things that don't come back.
- Damage can leave permanent scars (on characters, on places, on objects).
- Recovery does not erase what happened. It proves survival.
- The book respects its audience by not pretending the world is safe.

This policy applies to the novel series.
Brand-level materials (toys, parks, animation pitches) may maintain a stricter safety standard.


### THREAT TONE: Cosmic fungus should feel like: - mold infecting life - smoke that thinks - patient, inevitable It is creepy, not comedic. It is intelligent, not loud. HUMOR: - Light humor is allowed, mostly from Mina/Kai's competitive banter and ARC-7's stiff logic. - Humor should not break tension. - Tender moments (friendship, play, banter) need their own space and rhythm. - The story needs quiet human beats between the awe and the dread. VISUAL RULES: - When Zaro is weak or depressed: world desaturates slightly. - When fungus is near: ambient sound drops + wet-static rises. - Dome activation has a clear "lock" sound. SAKUGA RULE: - Every chapter must contain a hook within the first 1-2 pages and a major scene peak in the final third. - See Book Adaptation Guidelines and Prose Execution Guide for details. - For future animation: every episode will have 2 animation peaks (small early, big near the end) derived from the book's chapter structure.

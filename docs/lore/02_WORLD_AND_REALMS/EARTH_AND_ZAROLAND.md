## EARTH IN ZAROVERSE

Earth is mostly like our real world:

- cities, streets, phones, cars, modern life

But there is a hidden place where nature feels deeper and quieter.
That hidden place becomes Zaro's base.

## THE GREENBELT

The forest area outside the city is called The Greenbelt.
A normal name that people might use in real life.
Not famous. Not magical at first.
Simply a quiet green zone where people don't go often.

- Forest type: Mixed deciduous (oaks, maples, birch, wild cherry).
- Forest floor: ferns, moss, wildflowers.
- Creeks wind through the terrain.
- Old logging roads (unpaved, overgrown) serve as the only paths in.
- Faded "No Trespassing" signs — defunct private land.
- No cell service deep inside. No marked trails. No regular visitors.

## THE SEASON — EARLY SUMMER (JUNE) — LOCKED

Book 1 takes place in early summer (June). School just ended.
Duration: approximately 3 weeks (18-21 days). Season does not change.

What June in the Greenbelt looks like:

- Full green canopy at peak leaf density
- Wildflowers: black-eyed susans, daisies, clover, honeysuckle
- Creeks running clear but lower than spring snowmelt
- Long daylight: sunrise ~5:30am, sunset ~8:30pm
- Warm mornings, hot afternoons, cool evenings
- Fireflies at dusk (natural to deciduous forests in June)
- Birdsong constant, especially at dawn
- Smell of warm earth, cut grass, honeysuckle
- Occasional summer thunderstorms

Why early summer matters:

- Longest days = maximum sunlight for Zaro recovery
- Forest at peak fullness = dense cover, hidden world
- Summer vacation = Mina and Kai have all-day freedom
- Mina and Kai have natural freedom of movement June through September
- When Ink corrupts peak-green forest, contrast is devastating
- "First week of freedom" energy matches adventure tone

## ZARO'S HOUSE

An old forester's cabin in the forest.
One story, small porch, stone foundation, close to a creek.
Structurally sound but weathered and forgotten.
Zaro slowly restores it using his light:
warms the wood, seals cracks, clears dust, makes it cozy and safe.

Inside: Zaro has drawings and plans on the wall —
normal plans like a builder's notebook.
Where he wants paths and gardens, swings and tree houses later.
This proves Zaro is building a life, not just hiding from war.

The Treasures Shelf holds small personal objects he's found and restored.

## THE DOME

Zaro creates a protective boundary around the home space.
A light-based field that depends on his strength.
When Zaro is strong: dome is strong.
When Zaro is weak or sad: dome can flicker.

**DOME FILTER RULE:**

- Active Ink = blocked
- Normal debris = passes through
- Dormant/hibernating Ink = NOT filtered (Trojan path)

Human perception of the dome:
Normal humans can faintly perceive it as a warm shimmer —
like heat-haze on a summer road. Easy to dismiss.
They cannot perceive the exact boundary, connection to Zaro,
or any colored light.

## HOW ZARO CHANGES THE GREENBELT

Zaro's presence subtly amplifies the forest:

- Wildflowers grow thicker near the dome
- Creek runs clearer
- Moss is greener
- Air feels warmer inside the dome's range

Not dramatic enough for a casual visitor to notice.
Instruments can detect the gradient.

When the Ink arrives, this reverses:

- Affected areas lose color
- Wildflowers wilt and go gray
- Creek smells metallic and stale
- Birdsong stops. Animals go quiet.

## THE CONTAINER CRASH

Zaro's container crashed in the Greenbelt.

- Visible as a bright streak at night
- Local news reported "suspected meteor"
- Forestry service investigated within 48 hours
- Found scorched patch, displaced earth, no metallic fragments
- Container had dissolved
- Classified as small meteorite, no significant impact
- Report filed, no follow-up

Why dismissed: small meteors are common, remote location,
no damage or injuries, no conventional debris,
Spark Dust looks like mineral deposits.

## THE PORTAL CLEARING

Near the house, a clearing with a ring of stones.
Above the stones: a faint scar in the air (dormant).

When ARC-7 activates it:

- Air tears open
- Teal light explodes out
- Violent tunnel forms

The Veil tunnel is PERMANENT once torn.
Like a pipe between worlds. Lid can be shut, rip never heals.

# Visual Motifs and Symbols

These repeatable motifs help recognition across episodes, books, posters, merchandise, and games.

## Zaro Motifs (Yellow Prime)
- Bright yellow glow
- Spark particles floating like embers
- Warm ring shapes (shield, dome)
- "NOT OVER" written as a power trigger
- Tiny gray scar when he overdrives too hard

## ARC-7 Motifs (Cyan Prime)
- Teal / cyan geometry (clean lines, sharp shapes)
- HUD screens, warning dots, scanning beams
- Rule icons and system alerts
- Precision sealing patterns
- Overclock look: eye shift + lightning leak

## Black Hole Motifs (K Prime)
- Black hole circle
- Light bending at the rim
- Silence + slow motion
- Inward pulling motion
- Cold faint rim flare when it "notices"

## Cosmic Fungus / Ink Motifs
- Ink-smoke fog
- Mold-like crawling threads
- Stains that spread when not watched
- Wet-static sound texture
- Dots on screens multiplying quietly

## Mina / Fireflies Motifs
- Neutral white swarm
- Tight formations: ring, dome, line
- White-blue electric arcs when clustered
- Fingertip glove + bracelet glow

## Kai / MagDust Motifs
- Iron sand rising from the ground
- Heavy plates forming with a hum
- Thick glove pulse lines
- IRON BALL charge + smash + cooldown

## MemeWars Motifs
- Fighting game arena vibe
- Dark stages with fog/rain/steam
- Cold HUD words: READY, BEGIN, CLEAR
- Frame-readable impact moments

## Magenta Prime Motifs (tease only in Season 1)
- Sharp magenta pulse
- Clean but strange geometry (not ARC style)
- A new kind of light "logic" that feels alien

These motifs should appear again and again so audiences recognize ZaroVerse instantly.

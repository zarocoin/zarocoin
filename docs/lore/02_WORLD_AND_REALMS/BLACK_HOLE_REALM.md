# BLACK HOLE REALM (WORLD NOTES)


WHAT THIS REALM IS
This realm is not like Earth or ARC.
It is not a city or a planet.
It feels like deep space where light is being pulled inward.

THE BLACK HOLE (K PRIME)
The ruler presence of this realm is the Black Hole.
It is like a living black hole.
It does not need to shout to feel powerful.
Its power is gravity, hunger, and silence.

HOW IT LOOKS
The Black Hole should feel like:
- darkness that bends stars
- a circle where nothing escapes
- a rim that distorts light
Sometimes it may show a “gaze” or eye-like focus,
but it stays mysterious in Season 1.

SURVIVAL RULE
The Black Hole survives by stealing light.
If it cannot steal, it begins to fade and break apart.
That makes it hungry and strategic.

COSMIC FUNGUS (THE INK)
Cosmic Fungus is how the Black Hole reaches other worlds.
It spreads like:
- smoke
- mold
- spores
- crawling root threads

It tries to find light sources and drain them.

SENSE SYSTEM
The fungus is not only a weapon.
It is also the Black Hole’s senses.
When the fungus touches a world,
the Black Hole can feel what it feels.

Like a nervous system across space.

That is how the Black Hole:
- knows where Zaro went
- notices major signals
- reacts to events like “NOT OVER” and Green

ATTENTION MOMENTS (NO DIALOGUE)
We show the Black Hole’s attention with:
- a rim flare
- a slow pull ripple
- a subtle bend in background stars
It feels calm, but deadly.

WHY IT IS SCARY
Because it is not just angry.
It is patient.
It waits and advances quietly.
When it gets a path into another realm,
it does not celebrate — it simply spreads.

# Visual Motifs and Symbols

These motifs repeat across episodes, art, merch, games, and media.

They are the visual language of ZaroVerse.

---

## Zaro (Yellow Prime)

- warm yellow glow
- floating spark particles
- circular shield shapes
- handwritten-style power words
- subtle gray scar after overdrive

---

## ARC / ARC-7 (Cyan Prime)

- cyan and teal geometry
- clean, angular lines
- HUD overlays and warning symbols
- scanning beams and grids
- lightning leaks during overclock

---

## Black Hole (K Prime)

- circular void shapes
- light bending at edges
- silence and slow motion
- inward pull visuals
- cold rim glow when active

---

## Cosmic Fungus / Ink

- ink-like fog
- crawling thread patterns
- spreading stains
- static, wet sound textures
- multiplying screen artifacts

---

## Mina (Fireflies)

- white or pale-blue swarms
- precise formations
- synchronized movement
- glowing bracelets and gloves

---

## Kai (MagDust)

- iron sand motion
- heavy, grounded impacts
- deep hums and vibrations
- charge → release → cooldown rhythm

---

## MemeWars

- arena-style stages
- fog, rain, steam
- HUD words like READY and BEGIN
- frame-perfect impact shots

---

## Magenta Prime (Tease Only)

- sharp magenta pulses
- unfamiliar geometry
- light logic that feels wrong
- limited exposure in Season 1

# NAMES AND TERMS


These words are locked and stay consistent across all writing.


SEASON:
- Season 1


MAIN DARK SOURCE (K PRIME):
- THE BLACK HOLE
Description: A black hole-like presence that consumes light.
Short rule: The Black Hole survives by stealing light.


THREAT FORCE:
- COSMIC FUNGUS (official label on screens)
- THE INK (casual name used by humans)
Short rule: Humans say "Ink." ARC UI says "Cosmic Fungus."


ZARO:
- ZARO
Description: Yellow Prime, living spark of light condensed into a body.
Short rule: Zaro is light in a form.


ARC-7:
- ARC-7 (full designation: ARC PRIME 7)
Description: Cyan Prime watcher from the ARC Realm. One of seven Board members of the ARC Realm. Holds Rule 7 ("Do not cross worlds"). Occupies the warmest Cyan sub-band (the band most prone to drift). Broke his own rule. Kept his name.
Short rule: ARC-7 has strict rules. Breaking them has a cost.
Communication rule: ARC-7 does not waste words. When he speaks, every word carries weight. He never chit-chats, jokes, or over-explains.


OTHER ARC PRIMES:
- ARC PRIME 1 through ARC PRIME 6 — the six other Board members.
  Referenced collectively as "the Board" or "the remaining Primes."


ARC GENERAL:
- THE GENERAL
Class: GENERAL — third tier in the ARC hierarchy (between Prime and Soldier).
Description: White marble armor, larger build, diamond chest insignia.
Dispatched by the Board to find ARC-7 after his Rule 7 break. Cured and freed by the Amplified Verdant Seal. Follows ARC-7 home through the Veil by choice.
Short rule: Outranked by Primes. Capable of independent judgment within orders.


HUMAN PARTNERS:
- MINA (Fireflies operator)
- KAI (MagDust operator)


MAGENTA PRIME:
- Canonical name: MAGENTA PRIME
- Gender: Female
- Teased: passive (daylight aurora visibility over Greenbelt) and stinger (active — beam fired from deep space, dusk aurora flash arrival on Earth)
- Short rule: She completes CMY. She is what the team is missing.


SPECIAL PHRASE:
- NOT OVER
Short rule: When Zaro chooses defiance at his lowest point, it becomes a signal wave. Pure willpower, not magic. The carved symbol is the visible mark; the words live only in Zaro's mind.


SPECIAL COLOR:
- GREEN
Short rule: GREEN appears only when Yellow and Cyan spiral fast enough.


FUTURE COLORS:
- RED (M+Y): Passion meets warmth. Intense but volatile.
- BLUE (C+M): Logic meets mystery. Cold but brilliant.
- WHITE (C+M+Y): The ultimate. Only force that neutralizes K.


TRAINING WORLD:
- MEMEWARS
Short rule: Virtual arenas built by ARC-7 and enhanced by Mina + Kai.


LOCKED TERMS:

- OVERCLOCK: Correct spelling. ARC-7 pushes past safe limits.
- TREASURES SHELF: Small collected objects Zaro has found and restored — personal, sentimental, proof he is building a life. Separate from the fruits.
- TROJAN OBJECT: The object Zaro brings inside the dome willingly, carrying dormant Ink in hibernation. An amber crystal — naturally beautiful, catches light, refracts Zaro's spark into colors. The Ink hid dormant spores inside (look like natural marble veins). Zaro found it, thought it was natural, brought it inside. Beauty was the trap.
- ZAROLAND: The team's name for the Greenbelt forest once they've built something together.
- THE GREENBELT: The forest area outside the city. Mixed deciduous. Remote, isolated, no cell service.
- VERDANT SEAL: The Green finishing action. Controlled version (Zaro + ARC-7) and Amplified version (full team + tree).
- GLOW BURN: Zaro's stamina crash. Overuse of power drains him. Recovery requires rest and sunlight.
- SEED CORE: The living seed Zaro plants. Grows into the first tree. Produces fruits over time (not seeds — fruits contain no seeds inside).
- SPARK DUST: Golden glitter residue from Zaro's crash landing. Inert to humans. Detectable by instruments.
- MAGDUST: Magnetically reactive Spark Dust residue. Dark iron color. Kai controls it with the MagDust Glove.
- MAGDUST GLOVE: Kai's big thick EM glove. Houses electromagnetic pulse generator. Co-built with ARC-7.
- FIREFLIES: Mina's system — bracelet + finger rig + sensor drones + AR glass. The sensor drones look like fireflies.
- VEIL: The boundary between worlds. Once torn, creates a permanent tunnel.
- STONE RING CLEARING: Where ARC-7 tears the Veil. Near the house. Becomes permanent portal.
- NOT OVER SYMBOL: A universal visual mark from the CMYK system. Not text. Carved into the tree. Glows forever.
- ARC MESSENGER: Soldier-class courier units (background context).
- VERDANT SYNC: The visual signature of ARC-7 mid-sync, the instant before Yellow + Cyan colors merge into Green.
- BOOP: A small wood squirrel that becomes a recurring presence in the Greenbelt. Zaro names her Boop. She hides from ARC-7 and returns warily.
- THE BOARD: The 7-seat governing body of the ARC Realm. ARC Prime 1 through ARC Prime 7. Each Prime holds one Rule and occupies one Cyan sub-band.

# POWER RULES LOCK (CANON)

Changelog:
v2.1 — Added NOT OVER medium-specific note. Added Red/Blue/White combo placeholders. Added dome inverse law reference.

These rules prevent power confusion and keep the story believable.

## ZARO POWER (YELLOW PRIME)

Zaro is a spark being.
He can glow, shield, push, and restore.
He is effective alone.
But if he pushes too hard, he suffers Glow Burn (stamina crash).

### Zaro key truth

Breathing helps calm him.
But his brain is what truly ignites the spark.
Later he learns mental control.

### Zaro lowest state

His yellow fades to gray-yellow.
His dome weakens (see Dome Rules for inverse size/strength law).

### NOT OVER moment

When Zaro chooses NOT OVER at his lowest point:

- He snaps back instantly.
- Then he surges into OVERDRIVE.
- Sparks fly off him like overflow.

In the book, the trigger is the CHOICE of defiance.
Zaro thinks "NOT OVER" — the phrase lives in his mind.
What he CARVES is a universal SYMBOL from the CMYK system (not text).
The symbol works in every language and every medium.
NOT OVER is pure willpower, not magic. Not memory. Not mystical.

## ARC-7 POWER (CYAN PRIME)

ARC-7 is advanced and precise.
He contains, stabilizes, traces, and seals.
He is effective alone.
Overclocking drains him like a battery.

### Rule Seven

ARC-7 is not supposed to cross worlds.
Breaking Rule Seven is a major story event.
ARC-7 breaks Rule Seven because the NOT OVER signal overwhelms his systems.

## GREEN POWER (CYAN + YELLOW)

Green is not random.
It is the result of:

- Zaro Yellow energy + ARC-7 Cyan energy
- spiraling faster and faster
- until it appears green

Only Zaro + ARC-7 create Green.
No one else creates Green.

## FUTURE COMBOS (v2.1 — NEW, details locked for Book 2+)

- RED (M+Y): Passion meets warmth. Intense but volatile.
- BLUE (C+M): Logic meets mystery. Cold but brilliant.
- WHITE (C+M+Y): The ultimate neutralizer. Only force that can end the Black Hole.

## BLACK HOLE (K PRIME)

The Black Hole cannot be neutralized fully by Green.
It survives by consuming light.
K does not "combine" with other Primes. K only consumes.

## MINA AND KAI

Mina and Kai do not create Green, Red, Blue, or White on their own.
They do not seal cracks like ARC-7.
But they are NOT just support. Their gear can AMPLIFY the Verdant Seal:

- Kai's MagDust Glove can forge a pipe that focuses the Green beam
- Mina's Fireflies system can spark energy that supercharges the beam

When the full team works together (Zaro + ARC-7 + Kai + Mina + the tree),
the amplified Verdant Seal is exponentially more powerful than Green alone.
The team is greater than the sum of its parts.

# CMYK Prime System (Canon Lock)

ZaroVerse is built around four Prime forces based on real color science.

CMYK = Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black. Each one is a "Prime" — a rare core energy type with rules, limits, and costs.

## The Four Primes

### C = Cyan Prime
- Structure, precision, systems, containment
- This is **ARC-7**
- Teal/Cyan geometry energy

### M = Magenta Prime
- Teased at the end of Season 1 / Book 1
- Fully introduced in Season 2 / Book 2
- She is sharp, perceptive, magnetic, and unpredictable
- She completes the CMY triangle needed for White

### Y = Yellow Prime
- Hope, restoration, protection, willpower
- This is **Zaro**
- Warm yellow spark energy

### K = Black Prime
- Hunger, gravity, silence, theft
- This is **The Black Hole**
- Light does not escape it

## The Combo System

The CMYK system follows real color science. Every two-Prime combination produces a new frequency with unique properties, costs, and narrative meaning.

| Combo | Color | Meaning | Discovery |
|-------|-------|---------|-----------|
| C + Y | **Green** (Verdant Seal) | Logic meets warmth. Containment meets restoration | Book 1 finale |
| M + Y | **Red** | Passion meets warmth. Intense but volatile | Book 2+ |
| C + M | **Blue** | Logic meets mystery. Cold but brilliant | Book 2+ |
| C + M + Y | **White** (Ultimate) | All three Primes unified. The only force that neutralizes Black | End of Book 2 or Book 3 |

### Green (C + Y)
Neutralizes Cosmic Fungus in a space. Repairs and stabilizes. Green does NOT destroy the Black Hole — it only clears fungus and heals damage. Green is rare and must be earned. Only Zaro + ARC-7 create Green.

### Red (M + Y)
Powerful and emotionally charged, but unstable and hard to sustain. Red's volatility makes it dangerous — it can burn out or backfire. Full mechanics defined in Book 2 canon.

### Blue (C + M)
Strategic and precise, but emotionally detached. Blue lacks the warmth needed to truly restore — it can contain but not heal. Full mechanics defined in Book 2 canon.

### White (C + M + Y)
The ultimate neutralizer of the Black Hole. Requires all three Primes in perfect alignment. Enormous cost to all three. White is the endgame — it cannot be achieved without trust, sacrifice, and unity.

## What About K (Black) Combinations?

K does not "combine" with other Primes. K consumes. K steals. K absorbs.

If Black Prime energy touches another Prime's energy, it drains it, dims it, pulls it inward. There is no "Black + Yellow = dark yellow combo." There is only theft.

This is fundamental to the villain's nature: **The Black Hole cannot create. It can only take.**

## Important Rules

1. No random new colors — every color change must be explainable by CMYK science
2. Prime powers have limits — no infinite power
3. Green is rare — it must be earned
4. Black cannot be beaten by Green alone — that keeps the threat real
5. Red and Blue are volatile / limited — only White can end the war
6. Combos require real synchronization — they are earned through trust and timing, not by pressing two buttons

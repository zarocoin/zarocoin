# Color Rules (Canon Lock)

Color in ZaroVerse is not aesthetic decoration. It is semantic, energetic, and canonical.

## Core Principles

- A character's primary color does not change casually
- Color changes only occur through earned narrative events
- No character may freely swap color identity

## Prime Colors (CMYK System)

| Prime | Color | Meaning | Character |
|-------|-------|---------|-----------|
| Y | Bright Yellow | Hope, restoration, protection | Zaro |
| C | Teal / Cyan | Structure, precision, containment | ARC-7 |
| M | Magenta | Transformation (teased Season 1, introduced Season 2) | Unrevealed |
| K | Black | Hunger, gravity, silence, theft | The Black Hole |

## Zaro (Yellow Prime) — Color States

- **Bright Yellow** — normal, strong, stable
- **Dark Yellow** — tired, scared, strained
- **Gray-Yellow** — near collapse, almost empty
- **White-Hot Yellow** — Overdrive surge (rare)

When Zaro's light drops, the world can desaturate slightly.

## Combination Colors

| Combo | Result | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| C + Y | **Green** | Repair and neutralization — rare and dramatic |
| M + Y | **Red** | Passion meets warmth — intense but volatile |
| C + M | **Blue** | Logic meets mystery — cold but brilliant |
| C + M + Y | **White** | The ultimate — only force that neutralizes Black |

Green appears when Yellow and Cyan spiral fast enough to blend. Green does NOT neutralize the Black Hole — only White can.

## Gear Colors

- **Fireflies** (Mina): Neutral white glow, white-blue electric arcs. Never yellow, teal, green, or magenta.
- **MagDust** (Kai): Dark iron / metal gray. Must look physical and heavy, not magical.

## Forbidden Usage

- No random recoloring for style
- No multicolor "power-up" effects unless explicitly canon-approved
- No rainbow or spectrum blending without narrative cause

Color is lore. Breaking color rules breaks canon.

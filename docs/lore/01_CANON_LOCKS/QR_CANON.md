# # ZaroVerse QR Canon

Version 1.0

## Purpose
QR content in ZaroVerse books exists to deepen emotion and atmosphere,
not to advance plot or explain lore.

The book must always stand alone.
QR moments act as cinematic confirmations, not narrative requirements.

---

## Canon Rule #1 — Visual Source of Truth
All QR visuals originate from a single canonical master format:

• 16:9 widescreen image or video
• Cinematic framing
• No alternate crops generated for QR use

All adaptations for mobile viewing must preserve the original composition.

---

## Canon Rule #2 — QR Visual Structure
Every QR moment follows the same structure:

1. Visual (Primary)
   • 16:9 wide image or looping micro-video
   • Video duration: 5–8 seconds
   • Letterboxed presentation is allowed and preferred
   • Muted by default

2. Headline (Secondary)
   • Short
   • Poetic
   • Non-explanatory
   • Frames emotion, not information

3. Text Excerpt (Anchor)
   • 1–3 sentences from the book
   • Verbatim or lightly adapted
   • Written prose, not captions
   • Provides emotional grounding

---

## Canon Rule #3 — What QR Content May Show
QR visuals may show:
• Atmosphere
• Motion
• Light
• Environmental reaction
• Emotional state
• Consequence without cause

QR visuals must never introduce new plot facts.

---

## Canon Rule #4 — What QR Content Must Never Do
QR visuals must NOT:
• Advance the story independently
• Explain rules or systems
• Reveal future events
• Replace reading comprehension
• Function as cutscenes

The story always lives in the book first.

---

## Canon Rule #5 — Format Discipline
• No square or vertical-first compositions are created
• No QR-only artwork is generated
• All QR visuals are derivatives of canonical assets
• Visual silence and restraint are preferred over spectacle

---

## Canon Rule #6 — Reader Experience
The intended reader experience is:

Read → Pause → Scan → Feel → Return to reading

QR moments must feel like:
“A glimpse into something larger”
not
“extra content required to understand the story.”

---

## Canon Lock
This document defines the permanent rules for QR usage in ZaroVerse.
Any future changes require a versioned update.

Approved for Season 1 (Book 1), Chapters 1–2.

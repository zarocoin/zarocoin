# NOT OVER Signal Rules (Canon Lock)

"NOT OVER" is the signature emotional mechanic of ZaroVerse. It must stay consistent and earned.

## What NOT OVER Is

NOT OVER is not a magic spell. It is not a memory. It is not mystical. It is **pure defiance.**

When Zaro is at his lowest, his mind refuses to accept the end. The phrase arrives because he chooses it. No cosmic implant. No ancestral echo. Just a being who will not quit.

It acts like a heart restart shock.

## The Symbol

When NOT OVER manifests physically, Zaro does not write words. He carves a **symbol** — a universal visual mark from the CMYK system that means defiance.

The symbol is not text in any language. It is iconic and visual, designed to work across every language, every medium, and every adaptation. Think of it like Superman's S or Batman's bat — instantly recognizable without translation.

"NOT OVER" is what Zaro thinks. The symbol is what he creates.

## What It Does to Zaro

**Before NOT OVER:**
- Gray-yellow color state
- Weak body
- Dome flickers
- World begins to dim

**When NOT OVER happens:**
- Instant snap back to full yellow
- Sparks overflow like electricity
- Dome slams back online
- Zaro surges into Overdrive

**Cost:** Zaro gains a small gray scar. This proves the power-up has consequences.

## What It Does to the World

The wave travels outward like a pulse. It is strong enough to be sensed across realms.

**Who senses it:**
1. ARC-7's systems flood and alert — this triggers his overclock
2. The Black Hole feels it through the fungus network
3. Magenta Prime senses it from deep space

This is why NOT OVER is dangerous: it reveals Zaro's existence to bigger forces.

## The Tree Scar

When NOT OVER is carved into a tree, the mark is **permanent**. The scar never heals. One side of the tree is blackened forever.

But the symbol glows faintly — always. A low warm pulse powered by defiance and the Seed Core beneath. The tree will never be whole again. It will always show the blow. But it will also always show the recovery.

## What It Means

NOT OVER is Zaro's core philosophy: even when broken, he chooses to continue.

It becomes the franchise symbol. It becomes the audience rally point.

It must NEVER feel like lazy writing. It must only happen when the audience truly believes he is finished. Then it lands like a revolution.

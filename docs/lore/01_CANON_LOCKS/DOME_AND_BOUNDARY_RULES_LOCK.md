# DOME AND BOUNDARY RULES (CANON LOCK)


Changelog:
v1.1 — Added dome size/strength inverse law. Added body cost rule. Clarified dome does not cover external trees.

Zaro's dome is a light-based boundary field.
It is not magic without rules.


### WHAT THE DOME DOES


1) It blocks threats entering the home zone.
2) It stabilizes space around the house.
3) It keeps Zaro's base safe when he is strong.

The dome always has a clear "lock" sound when stable.


### WHAT THE DOME DOES NOT DO


1) It does not make Zaro invincible.
2) It does not block everything automatically.
3) It does not stay strong if Zaro is weak.
4) It does not extend to the trees or gardens outside the house perimeter.

The dome depends on Zaro's state.


### DOME SIZE / STRENGTH LAW (v1.1 — NEW)


The dome follows an inverse law between size and strength:

If Zaro has 100 units of energy:
- Small dome (house only): nearly impenetrable. Full strength.
- Expand the dome 10x: strength drops to ~10%. Thin, fragile, prone to breaking.

The wider the dome, the thinner the fabric.
Like stretching a balloon — cover more area, the walls get weaker.

This creates tactical decisions:
- Keep the dome tight and strong (house is safe, but trees and gardens are exposed)
- Expand the dome to protect more (everything gets weaker protection)

Zaro cannot protect everything.
This is the core tension of his defensive system.


### DOME COVERAGE (LOCKED)


Default dome: covers the house and immediate surroundings only.
The main tree (Seed Core tree) is planted OUTSIDE the dome's default range.
The smaller trees and gardens are also outside.

WHY THE TREE IS OUTSIDE (LOCKED):
- The dome has a fixed range. Zaro cannot choose to extend it without cost.
- The best planting soil is the impact crater area — rich with Spark Dust residue.
- The crater is just beyond the dome's natural edge.
- Zaro plants the Seed Core where it can grow best, not where it's safest.
- He doesn't have a choice. The dome covers the house. The seed needs open earth.
- The tree is ALWAYS outside the dome from the moment it's planted.
- This vulnerability exists from day one of the tree's life.

This means:
- The house is the safe zone
- Everything Zaro builds outside is vulnerable
- The tree — his most precious creation — is permanently exposed
- The fungus targets what is outside the dome first
- To protect the trees, Zaro must overextend — and pay the cost
- Every scene showing the tree carries low-level tension for the reader


### BODY COST RULE (v1.1 — NEW)


Dome creation and maintenance must always carry a physical sensation for Zaro.
The reader/viewer must feel that the dome COSTS something:
- Heat leaving his hands
- Pressure in his chest
- A slight dizziness when it first forms
- Fatigue after holding it for extended periods

Even when the dome forms smoothly, it is never free.
For the novel: if the dome feels effortless, the stakes collapse.


### HOW THE DOME WEAKENS


The dome weakens when:
- Zaro is drained (Glow Burn)
- Zaro becomes depressed or afraid
- Zaro is distracted
- Zaro is fighting inside and cannot maintain control

The dome does not "break instantly."
It flickers first.
This builds tension.


### WHO CAN ENTER THE DOME


1) Normal humans can enter physically if they walk in.
The dome is not a visible wall to normal people.
It is a field that stops harmful forces.

2) ARC-7 can enter because he is not hostile.
The dome does not treat ARC-7 as a threat.

3) Cosmic Fungus cannot enter in active form.

DOME FILTER RULE (LOCKED):
The dome filters active Ink and normal contamination.
- Active Ink, spores, threads — blocked
- Dirt, pollen, normal forest debris on shoes/clothes — passes through (harmless)
- Dormant/hibernating Ink — NOT filtered (appears dead, dome cannot detect it)
- The ONLY way Ink enters is in hibernation state, physically carried inside on an object
- Mina and Kai walking through the dome cannot accidentally carry active Ink inside
- But if hibernating Ink was on an object they carried, it could enter (same as crystal method)
- This means the Trojan method is the ONLY infiltration path — deliberate patience, not accident


### HOW A TROJAN GETS INSIDE (LOCKED)


Cosmic Fungus can enter only by hiding on an object.
A small dormant spore-trace can slip in because it is not active yet.

The iconic Trojan method (from Book 1):
A tool or object that promises to help Zaro build faster.
Something he CHOOSES to bring inside under time pressure.
Something that weaponizes his own good intentions.

Outside the dome:
it is asleep, dry, quiet.

Inside the dome:
warmth + energy wakes it up.
Then it spreads.

This is how the fungus wins:
not by punching the dome,
but by waiting and sneaking.


### AUDIO RULE (IMPORTANT)


Dome stable:
- clean "lock" sound

Dome weakening:
- low crackle / faint distortion

Dome failing:
- the lock sound stutters
- the air feels thinner

### ZARO (Yellow Prime)
- **Normal / Cinematic:** v1.7 — Golden living skin, pink cheeks, warm brown irises, chest glow, ember sparks. The skin register.
- **Gray / Near Death:** v7.3 — Ashen gray-gold skin, cheeks faded, sparks gone, barely-there edge glow. The audience should feel fear.
- **Supernova Surge:** v8.4 — Sakuga mid-surge frame. Smooth face, NO teeth, NO wrinkles. White-burning eyes carry all emotion. Mouth is a small tight line. Chest-burst light with crossing beams and lightning arcs. Pink cheek marks still readable through energy. The light screams, not the face.

**Zaro facial rules in extreme states:** NO teeth ever. NO wrinkles or frown lines. Smooth skin always. Emotion through EYES and body language only. Think Baymax/Kirby showing effort through minimal features.

### ARC-7 (Cyan Prime)
- **Default:** v2.5 — Compact humanoid robot, teal circuitry, dark matte armor panels. Built, not born.

- **Peak Overclock:** v2.12 — GOLDEN EYES (not teal, not white). Teal circuitry flickers between teal and gold. Dual-color lightning (teal from body, golden near eyes/chest). Armor FULLY INTACT — no cracks, no damage. Energy overflows through existing seams. THE GOLDEN SHIFT: Gold is Zaro's color bleeding into ARC-7's system — the machine learning to feel, expressed as light.

**ARC-7 overclock rule:** Armor NEVER cracks or breaks during overclock. The energy is too much for the body, not damaging the body. Overflowing, not destroying.

Asset #1 in the production queue. Blocks Tree States 3-5, sign-up page, and merch pipeline.

| State | Panel | Version | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Bud / Forming | Panel 1 | v12.9 | Tiny luminous sphere on branch, marble-sized, faint internal glow |

**Fruit design rules (see also FRUIT_RULES_LOCK.md):**
- **Perfect sphere** — geometrically round, unnaturally precise. Echoes ZARO Coin's perfect circle.
- **Self-illuminating** — glow from INSIDE. Translucent skin, not opaque. Living light source.
- **Internal light veins** — organic energy channels visible through skin. Diagonal crossings subtly suggest a Z to crypto viewers. NEVER a stamped logo.
- **Equatorial ring** — subtle raised growth line at the midline. Echoes ZARO Coin's double-ring border.
- **Luminous stem** — not woody. Made of the same light-material as the fruit.
- **NOT an Earth fruit** — no resemblance to apricot, peach, plum, apple, orange, mango, or any known fruit. Alien botany.
- **Golden-yellow matches ZARO Coin logo exactly** — not orange, not amber, not pale.

## ENVIRONMENT DESIGNS — LOCKED VERSIONS

### GREENBELT (Day/Night pair — April 2026)
- **v13.1 — Day:** Mixed deciduous forest in golden sunlight. Volumetric god-rays through full June canopy. Creek with smooth stones. Black-eyed susans, daisies, ferns, moss-covered fallen logs. Old stone wall consumed by growth. Bird on branch. Warm golden-green palette. No magic, no alien elements — pure Earth forest.
- **v13.2 — Night:** Same forest, moonlit. Cool blue-silver palette. Fireflies (natural Photinus pyralis) scattered through mid-ground — only warm elements in the cool scene. Owl on branch. Creek catching moonlight. Stars through canopy gaps. The quiet is the point.

**Greenbelt rules:** Real forest, NOT magical. Mixed deciduous (oaks, maples, birch). Early summer (June). Overgrown and forgotten — no paths, no signage, no maintenance. Day and Night are a matched pair — same composition, palette swap only.

### CABIN EXTERIOR (4-panel set)
- **v11.2 — Abandoned, Day:** Silver-gray wood, black void windows, warped porch, vines consuming. God-rays on emptiness. "The kind of quiet that happens when a place gives up expecting anyone."
- **v11.3 — Abandoned, Night:** Same decay, cool blue moonlight, all windows dark. Fireflies avoid the cabin. The loneliest image.
- **v11.4 — Occupied, Day:** Same bones but warm amber wood. Round window glows golden. Porch swept, small treasures on railing. Vines trimmed. Loved, not repaired.
- **v11.5 — Occupied, Night:** THE MONEY SHOT. Warm golden lantern in dark blue forest. Round window blazing amber. Ember sparks from roofline. Fireflies approach now.

**Cabin lore rule:** Human-built, abandoned long ago. Zaro FOUND it. Did NOT build it. Claimed it and made it home.

**Round window:** Canon signature detail. Mirrors Zaro's round form. Goes from black void (abandoned) to golden glow (occupied) — Zaro's whole story in one architectural detail.

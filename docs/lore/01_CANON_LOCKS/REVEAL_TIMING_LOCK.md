# REVEAL TIMING LOCK (CANON)


Changelog:
v2.1 — Updated from episode to chapter format. Updated gear timeline to match Book 1 outline. Updated Mina/Kai role to include Verdant Seal amplification.

This prevents random tools and powers appearing too early.
Every major reveal happens once and is earned.


### BOOK 1 REVEAL TIMELINE


CHAPTERS 1-2 (ARRIVAL + BUILDING):
- Zaro alone. Establishes his powers, dome, tree.
- Mina appears as a normal teen with civilian drones.
- Kai appears as a normal teen with maker skills.
- NO hero gear. NO combat. NO alien contact.

CHAPTER 3 (EXPEDITION):
- Mina's civilian drone detects anomaly.
- She and Kai investigate on foot.
- First glimpse of Zaro (cliffhanger).
- NO gear. Normal teens following curiosity.

CHAPTER 4 (FIRST CONTACT):
- Zaro, Mina, and Kai meet.
- Amber crystal found (Ink trap — reader sees the setup).
- NO combat. Connection and wonder.

CHAPTER 5 (BREACH — MIDPOINT):
- Crystal activates. Dome breached. Ink spreads.
- Zaro ALONE. Mina and Kai absent.
- NOT OVER symbol carved. Seed Core ignites.
- First Verdant Seal signal (beam reaches space).
- NO gear. Raw Zaro. Lowest point + defiance.

CHAPTER 6 (ARC-7 ARRIVES):
- ARC-7 tears the Veil. Arrives on Earth.
- Veil tunnel is permanent.
- Mina and Kai arrive to the aftermath.
- NO gear yet. Team forms.

CHAPTER 7 (TRAINING — GEAR REVEAL):
- ARC-7 creates Sim Arena → becomes MemeWars.
- GEAR BUILT HERE (co-built with ARC-7):
  * Kai's MagDust Glove (big, thick, EM-powered)
  * Mina's Fireflies system (bracelet + finger rig + sensors + AR glass)
- First real training sequences.
- Ink adapts (Perfect Copy, Wilted Garden).
- Team names the forest "ZaroLand."

CHAPTER 8 (DISCOVERY):
- Mina's Fireflies detect second Ink concentration.
- ARC-7 reveals Black Hole cosmic truth.
- Stakes jump from local to galactic.
- Battle plan set.

CHAPTER 9 (THE BATTLE):
- Full deployment of all gear in real combat.
- Each character faces their fear.
- CONTROLLED Verdant Seal (Zaro + ARC-7 only).
- Local victory. Arcs complete.

CHAPTER 10 (AFTERMATH + STINGERS):
- ARC-7 calls Messenger for supplies.
- Messenger infected → armored → aggressive.
- AMPLIFIED Verdant Seal (full team):
  Kai's pipe + Mina's spark + Zaro/ARC-7 Green + tree symbol.
- Messenger cured. Signal reaches Magenta + Black Hole.
- ARC-7 departs with hitchhiker Ink.
- Triple stinger (Magenta / Black Hole / Hitchhiker).


### IMPORTANT RULES


1. Gear does NOT appear before Chapter 7.
   Mina and Kai are normal teens with normal skills until then.

2. Controlled Verdant Seal (Ch9) before Amplified (Ch10).
   The team earns the bigger version.

3. Mina and Kai do NOT create Green on their own.
   But they CAN amplify it. The amplified version requires all four.

4. Every power reveal is earned through story pressure.
   Nothing appears because it's "cool." It appears because it's needed.

# NAMING EVOLUTION RULES (CANON LOCK)


This file locks how names evolve over time in Season 1 and beyond.
It prevents confusion and keeps the universe consistent.


### RULE 1 — THE GREENBELT → ZAROLAND


Early episodes:
The forest zone is called:
THE GREENBELT

This is what normal humans would call it.
It feels real and believable.

Later, after Zaro lives there and changes it:
- Zaro restores the house
- Zaro’s dome becomes active
- Spark Dust traces remain
- the tree grows and changes the area
- strange events begin spreading rumors

Then Mina and Kai name it:
ZAROLAND

Important:
ZaroLand is an earned name.
Not a starting label.


### RULE 2 — THE SIM → MEMEWARS ARENA


ARC-7 creates a sterile virtual system for training.
ARC-7 calls it:
THE SIM

ARC-7 designs it for function, not style.

Later:
Mina and Kai mod it like gamers.
They add:
- stages
- lighting
- music
- fighting game vibes
- UI flair

Then they rebrand it proudly:
MEMEWARS ARENA

Important:
MemeWars is not a joke.
It is the team’s arena name.
It becomes a franchise pillar and future game world.


### RULE 3 — COSMIC FUNGUS vs “THE INK”


Official technical name (on screens):
COSMIC FUNGUS

Casual human nickname:
THE INK

Why:
It looks like ink, smoke, stain, mold.
The nickname is simple and fast for dialogue.

Important:
We do NOT call it “pen magic” or “drawing powers.”
It is alive fungus-like spread.
It steals by draining, threading, and pulling light.


### RULE 4 — THE BLACK HOLE vs “K PRIME”


Villain presence name:
THE BLACK HOLE

Prime system label:
K PRIME

“Black Hole” is what characters say on Earth and ARC side.
“K Prime” is what lore and system files may call it in analysis.

Important:
We do NOT use “The Void” as the villain name.
The villain is The Black Hole.


### RULE 5 — SEASON NAMING


We call the first full season:
SEASON 1

Not Season 0.

This keeps it simple for the public.


### RULE 6 — TERMS MUST STAY SIMPLE


We use simple words that work globally.

Example:
Say “Black Hole” instead of complex myth names.
Say “Cosmic Fungus” instead of long cosmic titles.
Say “Greenbelt” instead of fantasy forest names.

Hard science can exist in the background,
but the dialogue stays clean and easy.


### FINAL CANON STATEMENT


Names evolve because the story evolves.
That evolution is part of the realism and the magic.

# COLOR RULES LOCK (CANON)


These colors are part of ZaroVerse identity. Do not drift.

PRIME COLORS (CMYK SYSTEM):
- Y = Yellow Prime (Zaro)
- C = Cyan Prime (ARC-7)
- M = Magenta Prime (teased end Season 1, introduced Season 2)
- K = Black Prime (The Black Hole)

ZARO (YELLOW PRIME):
- Icon color: BRIGHT YELLOW (warm, hopeful)
- Low state: DARK YELLOW (tired, scared)
- Near collapse: GRAY-YELLOW (almost empty)
- Overdrive: WHITE-HOT YELLOW (power surge)

Rule:
Zaro’s color shows his inner state like a life meter.
When his light drops, the world can desaturate slightly.

ARC-7 (CYAN PRIME):
- Icon color: TEAL / CYAN (cold, precise, clean)
Rule:
ARC-7 teal means containment and structure.

GREEN (C + Y COMBO):
- Green is rare and dramatic.
Rule:
Green appears when Yellow and Cyan spiral fast enough to blend.
Green repairs and neutralizes the cosmic fungus.
Green does NOT neutralize the Black Prime.

MAGENTA PRIME (M):
- Not fully shown in Season 1.
- Teased at end of Season 1.
Rule:
When Magenta appears, it must feel new, sharp, and powerful.

THE BLACK HOLE (K PRIME):
- Main look: BLACK HOLE DARK
- Rim distortion: faint cold glow, like bent starlight
Rule:
The Black Hole must feel like gravity, hunger, and silence.

MINA / FIREFLIES:
- Fireflies base glow: NEUTRAL WHITE
- Electric arcs: WHITE-BLUE (Tesla-like)
Rule:
Fireflies never look yellow, teal, green, or magenta.

KAI / MAGDUST:
- MagDust look: DARK IRON / METAL GRAY
Rule:
MagDust must look physical and heavy, not magical.

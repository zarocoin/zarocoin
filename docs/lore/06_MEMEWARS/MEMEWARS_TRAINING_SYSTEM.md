# MEMEWARS TRAINING SYSTEM (CANON)


MemeWars is ARC-7’s training simulation.
It is not a toy.
It is a serious system used to prepare for real threats.

ARC-7 built the base simulation (The Sim).
Mina and Kai modded it into MemeWars Arena.

Purpose:
Teach control, teamwork, timing, and stamina management
without destroying the real world.


### Training Philosophy (simple)


ARC-7 trains like a system:
- measure performance
- repeat drills
- increase difficulty
- expose limits safely

No long speeches.
Training is shown through action and tests.


### Zaro Training (Spark Control)


Main goal:
Zaro learns to control his spark without burning out.

Key truth:
Breathing helps calm him,
but the spark ignites from the mind.

Training drills:
- hold steady glow under pressure
- release small bursts without crash
- stop and start sparks on command
- maintain shield without shaking
- mental focus under fear (Silence Room)

Overdrive training:
Overdrive is powerful but dangerous.
Zaro must learn to enter it without losing himself.


### Mina Training (Fireflies Mastery)


Main goal:
Mina becomes stable under chaos.
She controls the swarm like a pro operator.

Fireflies drills:
- SCAN under a timer
- LIGHT path through obstacles
- AIR bubbles for targets in smoke
- CLEAN cycle after contamination

Advanced drills:
- SHIELD bubble save (60s cooldown)
- SPARK wave pushback (60s cooldown)
- keep formation under wind/rain interference

Important:
Mina is not “cute support.”
She is command under pressure.


### Kai Training (MagDust Control)


Main goal:
Kai builds structures fast and holds them under stress.

MagDust drills:
- build WALL / STAIRS / CEILING in seconds
- hold a brace while “earthquake” simulation hits
- DROP instantly and rebuild
- manage glove heat and stamina

Iron Ball drill:
IRON BALL = one heavy smash.
Then recharge 30–60 seconds.
Kai learns patience and timing.

Teamwork drill:
Kai breaks a hole with Iron Ball.
Mina sends Fireflies through to SCAN.
She relays safe paths back.

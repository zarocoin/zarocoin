# MemeWars Stage Cards (Game-Ready)

Quick reference for all Season 1 arena stages.

| # | Stage | Mood | FX | Sound |
|---|-------|------|----|-------|
| 1 | **Dojo Void** | Cold focus | Fog, echo | Deep pulse |
| 2 | **Iron Furnace** | Industrial danger | Steam, chains, orange glow | Machines + pressure hiss |
| 3 | **Neon Rain Rooftop** | Storm chaos | Rain, wind, neon flicker | Synth + thunder |
| 4 | **Rift Hall** | Unstable reality | Cracks, floating debris | Distortion + heartbeat |
| 5 | **Silence Room** | Mental pressure | Pulsing dim light, near silence | Breath + low hiss |
| 6 | **Black Rim Edge** | Watched by something huge | Star bending, inward pull | Deep pull tone |

**Note:** The villain realm is The Black Hole. Stage names can use "Void" as a mood word, but the lore name stays locked.

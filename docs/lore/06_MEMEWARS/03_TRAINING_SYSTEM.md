# MemeWars Training System

MemeWars is ARC-7's training simulation. It is not a toy. It is a serious system used to prepare for real threats.

ARC-7 built the base simulation (The Sim). Mina and Kai modded it into MemeWars Arena.

## Training Philosophy

ARC-7 trains like a system:
- Measure performance
- Repeat drills
- Increase difficulty
- Expose limits safely

No long speeches. Training is shown through action and tests.

## Zaro Training (Spark Control)

**Goal:** Zaro learns to control his spark without burning out.

**Key truth:** Breathing helps calm him, but the spark ignites from the mind.

**Drills:**
- Hold steady glow under pressure
- Release small bursts without crash
- Stop and start sparks on command
- Maintain shield without shaking
- Mental focus under fear (Silence Room)

**Overdrive training:** Overdrive is powerful but dangerous. Zaro must learn to enter it without losing himself.

## Mina Training (Fireflies Mastery)

**Goal:** Mina becomes stable under chaos. She controls the swarm like a pro operator.

**Drills:**
- SCAN under a timer
- LIGHT path through obstacles
- AIR bubbles for targets in smoke
- CLEAN cycle after contamination

**Advanced drills:**
- SHIELD bubble save (60s cooldown)
- SPARK wave pushback (60s cooldown)
- Keep formation under wind/rain interference

**Important:** Mina is not "cute support." She is command under pressure.

## Kai Training (MagDust Control)

**Goal:** Kai builds structures fast and holds them under stress.

**Drills:**
- Build WALL / STAIRS / CEILING in seconds
- Hold a brace while "earthquake" simulation hits
- DROP instantly and rebuild
- Manage glove heat and stamina

**Iron Ball drill:** IRON BALL = one heavy smash, then recharge 30–60 seconds. Kai learns patience and timing.

**Teamwork drill:** Kai breaks a hole with Iron Ball. Mina sends Fireflies through to SCAN. She relays safe paths back.

## Why MemeWars Matters Long-Term

MemeWars is not just training. It becomes a brand pillar:
- Story scenes can become game stages
- Moves can become game mechanics
- Codes and secrets can be hidden in arenas
- Fans can replay and analyze

It is a bridge: **story → game → community → rewards.**
